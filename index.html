Our weekly menu focuses around our love for organic, sustainable food and drinks. Our menu changes each Friday, focusing on whatever’s fresh and available to take advantage of local and in-season produce.

Something for Jess Cafe is located on the corner of Abercrombie and O’Connor Streets in Chippendale, 2kms south of the Sydney CBD. Eat in or take-away our delicious selection of baked treats, pies and sourdough sandwiches are made in house with love and care. Our menu caters to all whether you are big, small, vegetarian, vegan or gluten free.

Order coffee, breakfast or lunch straight to your home or work with Uber Eats and fast online delivery for the Sydney area.
